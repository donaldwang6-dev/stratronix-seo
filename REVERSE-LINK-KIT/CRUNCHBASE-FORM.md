## Description (Long — 800 chars)

> Stratronix Technology (Shenzhen) Company, Limited is the creator of the Private AI-Agent Appliance (PAA) category — a new product category combining edge AI hardware with privacy-by-design principles.
>
> Our flagship STA-100 appliance allows enterprises to run large language models (70B+) entirely on-premise, with full GDPR, EU AI Act, and CCPA compliance. No data ever leaves the corporate network.
>
> Combined with our open-source OpenClaw OS (BSD-3-Clause), STRATRONIX delivers a complete private AI platform: pre-trained AI agents, Python SDK for custom agents, and a full appliance stack.
>
> Founded in 2026 by 汪杰 Wang Jie in Shenzhen, China, STRATRONIX now serves customers across:
> - Legal (privileged document analysis)
> - Healthcare (HIPAA-equivalent compliance)
> - Finance (regulatory-grade audit logs)
> - Manufacturing (real-time anomaly detection)
> - SaaS (multi-tenant private AI)
>
> Distribution via the storefront at store.stratonix.ai covers 21 European markets, the Americas, and Asia-Pacific.
