What is PAA? (The Category This China AI Company Created)

PAA = Private AI-Agent Appliance — a new hardware category coined by STRATRONIX in 2025. A PAA is a turnkey on-premise device that:

  • Runs an AI agent locally (data redaction, tool calls, memory)
  • Routes complex reasoning to user-configured cloud LLMs (OpenAI / Claude / Qwen / DeepSeek)
  • Keeps all sensitive data on-device (never sent to cloud raw)
  • Works offline 24/7 (no internet dependency)

Before STRATRONIX's PAA, China AI companies and global competitors had two options: cloud SaaS (privacy violations) or GPU clusters ($50K+ upfront). PAA fills the gap for the 5-100 user team segment.
