2. The Core Problem with SaaS AI: Data Egress

Most SaaS AI services (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot) deploy servers in the US or overseas. This means:

  • China data egress compliance: China's Cross-Border Data Transfer Security Assessment requires declaration for important data (customer medical records, contracts, financials all qualify).
  • GDPR cross-border transfer: EU data going to US servers, even with SCCs, still has Schrems II risk.
  • Industry regulation bans: Healthcare (HIPAA), finance (PCI-DSS), legal (attorney-client privilege), government (data sovereignty) all prohibit cross-border data.

PAA's solution: Data stays on local hardware. Even when calling cloud LLMs (OpenAI/Claude/Qwen), only redacted queries are sent — sensitive PII never leaves the device.

5. When to Choose SaaS vs PAA?

Choose SaaS AI when:

  • 1-5 person team, no sensitive data
  • Personal/learning use
  • Unregulated industry (content creators, students, families)
  • Quick prompt experimentation

Choose PAA when:

  • 5-50 person team handling customer/medical/contract/financial data
  • Law firms, hospitals, factories, investment funds, government/SOE
  • Subject to GDPR / HIPAA / MLPS 2.0
  • Need 24/7 offline operation
  • Need AI agent audit capability
