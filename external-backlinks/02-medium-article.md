## The Problem: Enterprise AI Was Stuck Between Two Extremes

Before PAA, enterprises had 3 options for AI deployment, each with fatal flaws:

1. **Cloud SaaS (ChatGPT Team, $25/user/month)** — fast to deploy, but your data goes to OpenAI's servers. **GDPR violation**. Can't use for regulated industries (legal, healthcare, finance).

3. **Open-source agents (Dify, FastGPT)** — flexible, but require serious DevOps skills. Most non-tech enterprises can't deploy them.
